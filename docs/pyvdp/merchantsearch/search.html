Usage¶

pyvdp.merchantsearch.search.send(data)[source]¶

Submits a merchant search request.

Parameters:data (MerchantSearchData) – Required. Instance of MerchantSearchData().
Returns:A response from VDP.
Example:
from visa.merchantsearch.data import MerchantSearchData
from visa.merchantsearch import search

# See docs for MerchantSearchAttrList to find out how to build search_attrs
search_attrs = {
    'merchant_name': 'Acme Inc',
    'visa_merchant_id': 12345678,
    'visa_store_id': 123456789,
    'business_registration_id': 386004447,
    'merchant_url': 'http://www.emc.cmich.edu',
    'acquirer_card_acceptor_id': 424295031886,
    'acquiring_bin': 476197
}

response_attrs = ['GNSTANDARD']

search_options = {
    'max_records': 5,
    'match_indicators': True,
    'match_score': True,
    'proximity': list('Acme'),
    'wildcard': list('*cme*')
}

data = MerchantSearchData(search_attrs=search_attrs,
                          response_attrs=response_attrs,
                          options=search_options)

result = search.send(data=data)

class MerchantSearchAttrList(**kwargs)[source]¶

MerchantSearch searchAttrList data object model.

Part of MerchantSearchData object.

Parameters:
  • merchant_name (str) – Conditional. Name of the merchant. Optional when any one of VisaMerchantId or VisaStoreId or BusinessRegistrationId or MerchantUrl or AcquirerCardAcceptorId is provided.
  • merchant_street_address (str) – Conditional. Merchant street adress.
  • merchant_city (str) – Conditional. City of the registered merchant.
  • merchant_state (str) – Conditional. Merchant state code. 2 characters string.
  • merchant_postal_code (str) – Conditional. Merchant postal code.
  • merchant_country_code (int) – Conditional. Merchant country code, mandatory with merchant_name.
  • merchant_phone_number (int) – Conditional. Merchant phone number.
  • merchant_url (str) – Conditional. Merchant registered URL. Optional when any one of MerchantName or VisaMerchantId or VisaStoreId or BusinessRegistrationId or AcquirerCardAcceptorId is provided.
  • visa_merchant_id (int) – Conditional. Merchant ID provided by VISA. Optional when any one of MerchantName or VisaStoreId or BusinessRegistrationId or MerchantUrl or AcquirerCardAcceptorId is provided. 8 characters double.
  • visa_store_id (int) – Conditional. Merchant store/branch ID, provided by VISA. Optional when any one of MerchantName or VisaMerchantId or BusinessRegistrationId or MerchantUrl or AcquirerCardAcceptorId is provided. 9 characters double.
  • business_registration_id (str) – Conditional. Merchant business/tax registration ID. Optional when any one of MerchantName or VisaMerchantId or VisaStoreId or MerchantUrl or AcquirerCardAcceptorId is provided.
  • merchant_url – Conditional. Merchant registered URL. Optional when any one of MerchantName or VisaMerchantId or VisaStoreId or BusinessRegistrationId or AcquirerCardAcceptorId is provided.
  • acquirer_card_acceptor_id (str) – Conditional. Acquirer card acceptor ID. Optional when any one of MerchantName or VisaMerchantId or VisaStoreId or BusinessRegistrationId or MerchantUrl is provided. 15 digits string. Prepend 0 if less than 15 digits.
  • acquiring_bin (int) – Conditional. Acquirer business identification number. Required when AcquirerCardAcceptorId is provided.
